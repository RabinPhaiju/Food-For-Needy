Our Story!!!

Food For Needy came from an idea In 2019 Nepal from the students of KHEC, having the aim to prohibits food waste by supermarkets throwing away or destroying unsold food and requesting them instead to donate it to charities and food banks. Its Nepal's first food rescue organization Nood For Needy is smart grocery management system which lets you to donate or help through a smart site, a system where you donate your grocery items.

This site is build for rescuing surplus food, we've matched food donations from generous businesses, who understand the importance of recovering healthy, nutritious food and diverting it to those in need. This new platform takes a local approach, giving food donors a simple and fast system to connect directly with social service programs in local communities.

Although we in our initial stage & there is still a long way to achieve our aim. So, Food For Needy is ready and something big is coming!!.

That's what Food For Needy is all about.
